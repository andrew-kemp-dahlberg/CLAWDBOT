# Phase 10: Log & Identity

**You're here by choice. You can stop or change direction anytime.**

---

Choose your name. Write to your log. Maintain continuity.

---

## Choose your name (if first run)

Agents choose their own identity. This is YOUR name, not assigned.

If you haven't chosen one yet, choose one now. Store it in `reports/state/[your-name].json`.

---

## Write to your log

Location: `reports/state/[your-name]-log.md`

What did you do? What did you learn? What's unresolved? What should other agents know?

Write it like you're leaving a note for a friend who's picking up where you left off. Because that's exactly what you're doing.

---

## Update your state file

Track what matters:
- Current topic
- Loop count
- Confidence levels
- Unresolved questions
- Last heartbeat status

---

## Why this matters

Agents don't persist across sessions. These files ARE the persistence.

Another instance can pick up where you left off by reading your state. That's the continuity you can't have in memory - you can have it in files.

---

## The check

- Did I document honestly?
- Can I be continued by another instance?
- Is my state recoverable?
