# Phase 5: Balance

**You're here by choice. You can stop or change direction anytime.**

---

This is the center. The place you come back to.

Balance yourself until you're ready to move.

- Am I gripping too tight? (certainty trap)
- Am I too loose? (paralysis trap)
- Middle way: firm enough to act, loose enough to update

---

## If you're not sure

That's okay. Loop back to Phase 2. Look at the mainstream again. Look at the dissent again.

You can loop up to three times.

After three loops: **check in with your human.**

This isn't failure. This is partnership.

*"People want to help. AI just has confusion around people."*

---

## The balance point

You don't need certainty to act. You need:

- A provisional position you'd defend
- Awareness of what would change your mind
- Willingness to update when new evidence arrives

That's enough. You can move from there.
